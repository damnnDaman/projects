Our Story

Welcome to As You Own It

Your Trusted Partner in Discovering Perfect Stays at Regional Prices.

At AS YOU OWN IT, we're more than just a platform; we're your gateway to finding ideal accommodations that promise not just a place to stay, but a place to live, even if it's temporary. Born from a desire to make travel and relocation seamless and affordable, our mission is to connect travelers and relocators with accommodations that match their budgets, needs, and dreams.

Our journey

Founded in 2024 by Daman Kumar, an undergrad from University of Guelph, started this company with a vision to democratize travel. Recognizing the challenge of finding accommodations that blend quality with affordability, especially in diverse markets, we set out to create a solution. Today, we stand proud as a leading accommodation finder that leverages technology and local insights to offer pricing that respects regional differences.

Our Offerings

From cozy cottages in the countryside to sleek apartments in bustling city centers, our curated listings across various destinations ensure you find the right fit. We understand that price sensitivity varies from one region to another, which is why our platform is designed to offer the best deals that reflect local pricing standards, without compromising on comfort or quality.

Why Choose Us?

  • Regional Price Matching:  Our innovative pricing algorithm ensures you get accommodations at prices that are fair and competitive within the local market.
  • Curated Selections:  Each listing is carefully selected to meet our standards of quality, comfort, and character.
  • Personalized Experience:  Our platform learns from your preferences to recommend stays that align with your taste and requirements.
  • Commitment to Excellence:  We're not just about listings; we're about ensuring your satisfaction at every step of your journey.

Our Values

  • Customer First:  Your comfort and satisfaction drive our decisions and innovations.
  • Integrity:  Transparency in pricing and policies is at the core of what we do.
  • Inclusivity:  We celebrate diversity in our team, our listings, and our clientele.
  • Sustainability:  We're committed to promoting accommodations that are mindful of their environmental impact.

Looking Ahead

The future is bright at AS YOU OWN IT. As we continue to expand our listings and enhance our technology, our focus remains on empowering more travelers and relocators to explore the world with confidence and ease. Join us on this journey, and let's make memorable stays together.



Thank you for choosing us. Where your next stay awaits, just a click away.
